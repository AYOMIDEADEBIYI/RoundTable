Empowering leaders with faith and purpose

We are a thriving community of young christian leaders. We reconnect people of faith with God and inspire them to maximize their full potential; through prayer, word, worship, and early morning devotions.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND

Any young Christian with a desire to lead,serve, and grow in faith should attend. Our community welcomes all who seek to make a positive impact on the world while staying true to their Christian values.

WHY YOU SHOULD ATTEND

Attend to ignite your passion, strengthen your faith, and forge lifelong connections, while gaining the knowledge and tools to shape a brigher future for yourself and your community.

FIND YOUR TRIBE

You will meet your people, people who share your ambitions and are on the same journey as you. Make the most of interactive networking sessions to exchange ideas and create valuable life-long connections!

OUR SPEAKERS

The Roundtable gathers brilliant and successful speakers, entrepreneurs and trail blazers to candidly answer the fundamental question, “How did I make it?” so that you can be able to envision a clearer path towards how you will make it too. It is hosted by Olaoye Abiodun Oladimeji, CEO of HAC.

Olaoye Abiodun Oladimeji

Olaoye Abiodun Oladimeji is the Founder and Chief Servant of the Human Advancement Conversation Initiative.

Abiodun is a Project Manager and a Transformational Leader passionate about leadership and people development. He walks people through the deep levels of change, growth and transformation.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about The Roundtable 2.0

  • What is The Roundtable event about?

    The Roundtable is a 1-day event that provides a candid and relaxed atmosphere for young ambitious people seeking to accelerate their personal and professional journeys & connect with their tribe. This gathering fosters a safe space for participants to show up as their authentic selves and collectively work through the real life questions and issues young people face, with the aim of catalyzing their self belief and unearthing their deepest potential.

  • When and where will The Roundtable take place?

    It will take place on Saturday 25th, November 2023 in Lagos, Nigeria.

  • Who is The Roundtable for?

    For any young person who wants more: seeking personal and professional growth, young professionals across any industry, budding entrepreneurs and business owners, recent graduates or students in their final year of university. Come one, come all!

  • How do I purchase tickets for The Roundtable?

    Buy tickets here

  • How can I apply for the 1:1 mentorship session with Kennedy Ekezie?

    All registered attendees would receive emails with instructions on how to apply

  • Are there group discounts available for ticket purchases?

    Absolutely! Please email Kamsi at kamsi@kennedyekezie.com if you’d like to purchase group tickets for your organization or community

  • Can I attend virtually?

    Yes, you can! We will be offering tickets for virtual attendance soon. Sign up for our updates to be the first to know when we do.

  • What is the theme for The Roundtable 2.0?

    Our theme is Possibility! There are no limits to what you can accomplish.
